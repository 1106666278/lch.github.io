About This Addictive Crowd Runner

Count Masters: Stickman Games is a highly engaging and fast-paced casual runner game. Your primary objective is to gather the largest possible crowd of stickman followers as you navigate challenging levels filled with obstacles and enemies. It's a test of quick reflexes and strategic thinking!

Gameplay Mechanics Explained

The game starts with you controlling a single stickman. As you run down the path, you'll encounter gates with mathematical operations (+, -, *, /). Choose the gates that will increase your numbers the most and avoid those that will reduce your crowd. You'll also face rival stickman groups and environmental hazards that can decimate your followers. Reach the end with the largest crowd to defeat the boss and win the level!
